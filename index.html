MISSION STATEMENT

OUR MISSION IS ROOTED IN CHRISTIAN VALUES AND STRIVES TO BETTER THE LIVES OF ALL PEOPLE THROUGH PROGRAMS DESIGNED TO DEVELOP BODY, MIND, AND SPIRIT TO MEET ANY NEED. TOGETHER WE CAN BECOME POWERFUL AND EFFECTIVE THROUGH DEDICATION!

HERE AT JAMES 5:16 CENTER, TRAINING IS MORE THAN JUST PHYSICAL. WE ENCOURAGE PEOPLE OF ALL AGES, ESPECIALLY YOUNG PEOPLE, TO USE THEIR BODY, MIND AND SPIRIT IN POSITIVE WAYS.

WE OFFER POSITIVE CHARACTER BUILDING AND DISCIPLINED ACTIVITY TO YOUNG PEOPLE. OUR HOPE IS THAT WE WILL GIVE A KNOCKOUT PUNCH TO DRUGS, ALCOHOL, AND OTHER THINGS THAT TRY TO KEEP OUR YOUNG PEOPLE FROM BECOMING ALL THAT THEY CAN BE.
